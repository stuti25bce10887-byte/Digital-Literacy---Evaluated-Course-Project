In one situation, a student sent an email to a professor without a proper subject line or greeting, using informal language and incomplete sentences. This created confusion and gave an unprofessional impression. The professor did not respond quickly because the message lacked clarity and respect.

This situation could have been handled better by writing a clear subject line, using a polite greeting, and explaining the request properly. Professional communication is important because it reflects respect and seriousness. Using proper email etiquette ensures that the message is understood clearly and increases the chances of receiving a positive response.
