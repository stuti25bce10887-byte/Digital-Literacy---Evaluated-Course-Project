Stay Safe Online Checklist:

- Do not click on suspicious links
- Always verify website URLs before logging in
- Never share OTPs or passwords
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Regularly update apps and software
- Be cautious of unknown emails or messages

Financial/UPI Safety:
- Do not share UPI PIN with anyone
- Double-check payment requests before approving

Reporting Channels:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
